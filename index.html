Search Tips
  • Phrase search: Use quotes for exact phrases (e.g., "waiting for"). This will find all occurrences regardless of capitalization.
  • Exact matching: Short words (under 6 letters) use exact matching to avoid false positives (e.g., "table" won't match "cable")
  • Fuzzy matching: Add ~ after any word for fuzzy matching (e.g., table~ will match "cable", "stable")
  • Date search: Include years (e.g., 2011) or months (e.g., april, apr)
  • Long words: Words 6+ letters automatically use fuzzy matching for typos
  • Combine searches: Mix phrases and keywords (e.g., "waiting for" 2011)
